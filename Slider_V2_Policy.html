We only collect information about you if we have a reason to do so–for example, to provide our Services, to communicate with you, or to make our Services better.

We collect information in three ways: if and when you provide information to us, automatically through operating our services, and from outside sources. Let’s go over the information that we collect.

  • Transaction and Billing Information: If you buy something from us information is processed through the Google Play Store. While we do receive some important billing information, none of that information will be used for any purpose outside of billing and accounting uses.

Information We Collect Automatically

We also collect some information automatically:

  • Log Information: Like most online service providers, we collect information that web browsers, mobile devices, and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, IP address, unique device identifiers, language preference, referring site, the date and time of access, operating system, and mobile network information. We collect log information when you use our Services–for example, when you login or upload content to Slider.
  • Usage Information: We collect information about your usage of our Services. For example we collect information on when you login and when and how you interact with Content available on our services such as uploading content, viewing others content and interacting with content. We use this information to, for example, provide our Services to you, as well as get insights on how people use our Services, so we can make our Services better.

How We Use Information

We use information about you as mentioned above and as follows:

  • To provide our Services–for example, to set up and maintain your account, host your Content and backup and restore your Content.
  • To further develop our Services–for example by adding new features that we think our users will enjoy.
  • To monitor and analyze trends and better understand how users interact with our Services, which helps us improve our Services and make them easier to use;
  • To monitor and protect the security of our Services, detect and prevent fraudulent transactions and other illegal activities, fight spam, and protect the rights and property of Slider and others;
  • To personalize your experience using our Services, provide content recommendations and serve relevant advertisements.
  • To contact you if any specific account action is required such as a password reset, or a removal of Content.

How We Share Information

We do not sell our users’ private personal information. Account information provided to Slider is not shared outside of our Services and is only used for the purposes of managing a Slider account.

Security

While no online service is 100% secure, we work very hard to protect information about you against unauthorized access, use, alteration, or destruction, and take reasonable measures to do so.
